Common Questions

Is the inspection really free? +
Yes, completely free with zero obligation. If we find damage and you want us to handle the repair, we'll provide a full quote. If your roof is fine or you decide not to use us, you owe nothing.
What if my roof doesn't have damage? +
We tell you honestly. About 30% of roofs we inspect have no claimable damage — in that case we give you a written report on the current condition and a recommended maintenance timeline. Still completely free.
Will filing an insurance claim raise my rates? +
Storm and freeze-thaw damage claims are typically classified as "Acts of God" and do not count against your personal claims history in most Ontario policies. We'll walk you through your specific policy before you decide to file anything.
How long does the inspection take? +
Typically 20–40 minutes depending on roof size and pitch. You don't need to be home — just let us know and we can inspect while you're at work.
How soon can you come out? +
Most appointments are available within 2–3 business days. Submit your info above and we'll contact you within 1 hour to schedule a time that works for you.
