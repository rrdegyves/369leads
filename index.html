FAQ

Are leads exclusive?
Yes. We do not resell the same homeowner contact to multiple roofers in your territory.
What if I am already running Google Ads?
We audit what you have, fix tracking and landing gaps, and either take over or run alongside—whichever gets you cleaner cost per booked estimate.
Do you work outside storm season?
Yes. Retail replacement and repair campaigns run year-round; storm is layered when weather and search demand spike.
How fast can we start?
After a short fit call and market check, most partners are live within two to three weeks depending on creative and tracking setup.
Do Facebook ads still work for roofers with 2026 targeting rules?
Yes—when campaigns are set up under Meta’s Housing special-ad rules (which limit some old targeting tricks). We lean on strong offers, creative, and your speed-to-lead—not sketchy micro-targeting.
How does your cost compare to typical roofing lead costs?
Published industry bands for roofing often show higher cost per lead than many trades—we focus on exclusive intent in your territory and transparent math. Use our ROI calculator with your close rate and average job.
