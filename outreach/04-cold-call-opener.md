# Cold call — 45-second opener

“Hi, is this [Name]? This is [Your name]—quick question: do you handle marketing for [Company], or is there someone else I should speak with?”

*(If correct person:)*

“I work with roofing companies in [region] on **exclusive Facebook leads**—homeowners asking for **free roof inspections**, not shared with four other contractors. I’m only taking **one shop per city** right now—is [City] something you’re still trying to grow this year?”

*(If yes or maybe:)*

“Perfect—I’m not trying to sell you on the phone. Can we book **15 minutes** tomorrow so I can show you the ROI math and territory map? I’ll send a one-pager after.”

**If objection “send email”:** “Happy to—what’s the best address? I’ll send the proposal deck and a few times that work for a short call.”
