# Objection handlers (short)

1. **“How do I know lead quality?”**  
   That’s why the guarantee exists—and we define “qualified” in writing on day 1. My business only works if you close.

2. **“We use HomeAdvisor.”**  
   Those leads are shared; mine aren’t. Effective CPL often beats shared marketplaces when you factor contact rate.

3. **“No budget.”**  
   One closed roof job typically covers **months** of the retainer. If we hit the guarantee, the math is the budget.

4. **“Facebook didn’t work before.”**  
   Was it boosted posts or a real lead campaign with a dedicated offer page? I’ll make sure we don’t repeat the same mistake.

5. **“Pay per lead?”**  
   We can discuss, but retainers align us to **optimize the system**—not argue every month over counting.

6. **“One-month trial.”**  
   Two-month minimum: week 1 build, weeks 2–3 data, week 4 optimization. One month isn’t a fair test.

7. **“Need to think / ask partner.”**  
   What’s the main uncertainty—results, price, or timing? Territory isn’t infinite; I can hold until [date] if helpful.

8. **“Too expensive.”**  
   What are you spending now per job acquired? *(pause)* Does **$2,500** still look expensive against **~$55k+** in modeled revenue if you close **around five** roofs at typical ticket?
