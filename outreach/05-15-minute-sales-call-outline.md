# 15-minute discovery call outline

## 0–3 min — Qualify (they talk)

- “How are you getting customers today?”
- “How many new **roofing jobs** per month do you **need** to hit your revenue goal?”
- Listen for: Angi/HomeAdvisor, bad FB experience, capacity, speed-to-lead

## 3–8 min — Offer (you talk less than you think)

- Meta lead gen → **exclusive** territory → you handle build/optimize → they call fast
- Name the tier that fits: Starter (test), Growth (main), Domination (volume + systems)

## 8–12 min — ROI math (out loud)

Example Growth: “We plan for about **20–25 leads** a month in range—if we model **22** at **25% close**, that’s **five or six jobs** × **$11k**. Your close rate might be higher or lower—we track reality in week 3–4.”

## 12–15 min — Guarantee + next step

- State tier guarantee (10 / 20 / 25 qualified leads in first 30 days or month 2 half off)
- “I need a yes to **lock the territory**—I’ll send agreement + payment link today.”

**After call:** Send matching deck PDF + calendar for kickoff.
