# Client onboarding checklist — 369 Leads (roofing)

Use from the moment a prospect says yes through day 7 live. Aligns with *Roofing Lead Gen Agency Business Plan 2026*.

## Before first dollar clears

- [ ] Signed agreement (tier: Starter / Growth / Domination) + qualified lead definition initialed
- [ ] Stripe (or processor) recurring charge scheduled for day 1 of each month
- [ ] Kickoff call booked within 48 hours of payment

## Day 1 — Collect from client

- [ ] Facebook Page — admin/partner access via Business Manager (no password sharing)
- [ ] Service area — ZIP list or mile radius from office (map screenshot saved)
- [ ] Google Business Profile URL + current review count (for proof on landing)
- [ ] Primary phone for lead alerts + backup contact
- [ ] 3–5 before/after job photos (license to use in ads)
- [ ] Confirmed homeowner offer wording (“Free roof inspection,” etc.)
- [ ] License # and insurance proof if required for ads disclaimers in your state

## Day 1–3 — You build

- [ ] GHL sub-account (or agreed stack): `[Client] — [City]`
- [ ] Pipeline stages: New Lead → Contacted → Appt booked → Quoted → Won → Lost
- [ ] Form fields: name, phone, address, homeowner, roof age, concern (+ email optional per plan)
- [ ] Instant SMS to homeowner + internal notify to client (test with dummy submit)
- [ ] Meta Pixel + Lead event on thank-you (if using landing page)
- [ ] 3 ad creatives: 1 video or carousel concept, 1 before/after, 1 text-on-image
- [ ] Targeting doc saved: radius, age 35–65+, homeowner layer, income if used

## Day 3–7 — Launch

- [ ] **Meta:** Campaign uses correct **Special Ad Category (Housing)** per `operations/05-meta-special-ad-category-housing-compliance.md`
- [ ] Client Loom: “Where leads appear + how to move pipeline stages”
- [ ] Speed-to-lead verbal commitment recorded (5–10 min target)
- [ ] Ads live OR lead forms live per tier (Starter may start Lead Form–only)
- [ ] Weekly reporting channel confirmed (email, Slack, or GHL notification)
- [ ] Client sent **SLA** snippet from `operations/07-agency-SLA-to-clients.md` (response-time expectations)

## Day 7 — Sign-off

- [ ] Client confirms they received test lead end-to-end
- [ ] Next bi-weekly or weekly call on calendar (per tier)
- [ ] Internal note: CPL baseline, tier, guarantee threshold (15 / 30 / 50 leads in 30 days)

---

**Qualified lead (default — customize in contract):** Homeowner (or authorized for owner), property in agreed territory, submitted request for free roof inspection within tracking window (typically 7 days).
