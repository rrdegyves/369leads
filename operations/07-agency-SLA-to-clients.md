# Agency SLA to clients (publish internally + in welcome pack)

**Purpose:** Set **response expectations** so clients don’t panic-chargeback when they’re bored on a Sunday. Tune hours to your real schedule.

---

## Standard commitments

| Type | Our commitment |
|------|----------------|
| **Ads down / disapproved / account restricted** | You acknowledge same **business day** (define your TZ + hours); first fix or comms within **24h** of notice |
| **CPL spike / lead drop >40% vs 7-day avg** | Proactive Loom or email by **next business day** with hypothesis + action |
| **Routine questions** (Slack/email) | **24 business hours** |
| **Scheduled strategy calls** | Per tier; reschedule if needed **48h** notice preferred |

## Not included (say explicitly)

- **24/7** monitoring of ad account  
- **Instant** changes to creative on **nights/weekends** unless **retainer add-on**  
- **Guarantee** of **Meta** uptime, **GHL** uptime, or **homeowner** behavior

## Client-facing one-liner (paste in welcome email)

“We’re fast on **breakages** (same business day when possible). For **everything else**, expect a **24-hour business-day** response—we’re lean on purpose so your retainer goes to **ads**, not overhead.”

---

*Adjust if you hire a VA for weekends.*
