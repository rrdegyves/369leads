# Kickoff call agenda (~30 minutes)

## 0–5 min — Expectations

- Week 1 = build + QA. Week 2–3 = data. Week 4+ = optimization.
- “By day 30 you’ll have a clear picture of volume and CPL; guarantee is tied to **qualified** leads we defined in writing.”

## 5–10 min — Speed-to-lead (non-negotiable)

- “New leads need a call in **5 minutes** when possible. After 30 minutes, contact rates fall off a cliff.”
- Who answers: owner, office, ISA? After-hours voicemail or text?
- Write down the exact number that gets pinged on every lead.

## 10–20 min — Tool walkthrough

- Show GHL (or stack): incoming lead → pipeline stage → SMS log
- How to mark Contacted / Appt booked (so you can report honestly)
- Where they’ll see weekly/monthly summaries from you

## 20–25 min — Qualified lead (read aloud & align)

- Confirm: homeowner, territory, inspection request
- Agree what is **not** qualified (renter, wrong ZIP, spam, duplicate within X days)

## 25–30 min — Next steps

- Book first strategy check-in (bi-weekly for Growth/Domination; as sold for Starter)
- Book **30-day review** before you hang up (retention + upsell slot)
- Confirm ad spend budget if separate from retainer (if applicable)

---

**One-liner to close the call:** “If anything looks off in the first two weeks, text me—we fix fast. Your job is to call leads fast; mine is to feed you enough of the right ones.”
