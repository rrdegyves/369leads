# Weekly fulfillment rhythm (~6 hrs/client/week at scale)

## Monday (~1.5 hrs per client block)

- Review CTR, CPL, frequency, spend pacing
- Pause ads under ~1% CTR after meaningful impressions; scale winners +20% every few days if CPL holds

## Wednesday (~1 hr)

- Export leads from GHL; note qualified vs junk
- Send short Loom per client: “This week’s volume, what I changed, what I’m testing next”

## Thursday (~2 hrs creative)

- Build next week’s variants from Meta Ad Library notes
- Refresh anything running 3+ weeks with fatigue

## Friday (~1.5 hrs)

- Run scheduled strategy calls (rotate if bi-weekly)
- Flag churn risk: low contact rate, “leads are bad” without data — schedule coaching call

## Monthly

- Written report: leads, CPL trend, top creatives, next month plan
- Invoice / autopay reconciliation
- Domination: reputation + appointment-setting metrics as sold

---

**Internal KPIs:** CPL band (e.g. target &lt;$40 early), contact rate from client (goal 60%+), appointments booked if trackable.
