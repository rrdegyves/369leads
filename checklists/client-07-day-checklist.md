# First 7 days — client checklist

Use alongside Agency’s internal onboarding. Goal: **complete tasks so ads can go live ≤7 days after assets + access are complete.**

## Day 1

- [ ] Read welcome email; confirm **lead alert phone** receives test texts  
- [ ] Send **Business Manager** partner invite to Agency (exact email/ID they provide)  
- [ ] Confirm **which Facebook Page** ads will use  
- [ ] Reply with **ZIP list** or map screenshot of **exact** service area (no “whole state” unless agreed)  
- [ ] Attach **minimum 3 photos** for ads (high-res; no watermarks you don’t own)

## Day 2

- [ ] Send **logo** (PNG transparent preferred)  
- [ ] Send **Google Business Profile link** + current **star rating** and **review count**  
- [ ] Confirm **offer text** (“Free roof inspection” or approved alternate)  
- [ ] Attend **kickoff call**; confirm **who** calls leads when you’re on a roof

## Day 3

- [ ] Accept **GHL** (or agreed CRM) invite; log in once on **phone + desktop**  
- [ ] If Growth+: approve **domain** or add **DNS records** Agency sends (can take 24–48h)  
- [ ] Provide **license #** or “N/A per my attorney” if not used in ads

## Day 4–5

- [ ] Review **draft landing** (if applicable): spelling, phone, license line  
- [ ] Submit **one test lead** when Agency asks; confirm you get **notification** in &lt;2 min  
- [ ] Fix **call routing** if leads went to voicemail during test

## Day 6–7

- [ ] Confirm **ads are live** (Agency will notify)  
- [ ] **Do not** change **Page name**, **URL**, or **disconnect** BM access without telling Agency  
- [ ] Book **next check-in** (bi-weekly or weekly per tier)

## If you’re stuck

- **“I don’t know Business Manager”** — Ask Agency for a **60-second Loom**; it’s one invite.  
- **“No photos”** — Send **stock** isn’t ideal; use **supplier** or **shingle manufacturer** generic shots only if rights allow.  
- **“I’m too busy to call leads”** — Assign **one person** now; speed-to-lead is non-negotiable for ROI.
