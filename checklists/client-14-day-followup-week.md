# Days 8–14 — follow-up week checklist

**Purpose:** Early optimization window. Leads are flowing or about to; habits get locked in.

## Client — daily (business days)

- [ ] **Call/text every new lead** within 5–10 minutes when possible  
- [ ] Move deals in **CRM** to **Contacted** / **Appt booked** so reporting is honest  
- [ ] **Do not** blame “bad leads” before **3+ contact attempts** (call, text, VM)

## Client — one-time this week

- [ ] Send **1–2 new job photos** if Agency requests fresh creative  
- [ ] Confirm **after-hours** process: who gets leads nights/weekends?  
- [ ] List **top 3 objections** homeowners give you — Agency can tune ad copy

## Agency (you) — mirror checklist

- [ ] **CPL / CTR** review; pause obvious losers  
- [ ] **Loom #1** to client: week’s leads, what you changed  
- [ ] Confirm **pixel / form** still firing after any client-side site change  
- [ ] Schedule **strategy call** if Growth/Domination

## End of week 2 — 10-minute self-score (client)

| Question | 1–5 |
|----------|-----|
| I contacted ≥80% of leads same day | ___ |
| My CRM stages are up to date | ___ |
| I know where every lead came from (Meta vs other) | ___ |

If any score ≤3, fix **process** before judging lead “quality.”
