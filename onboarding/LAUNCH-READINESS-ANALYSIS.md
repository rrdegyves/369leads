# What clients must give you to run Meta ads ASAP

Analysis from your **2026 business plan**, **onboarding SOPs**, and typical Meta roofing campaigns. Use this as a **sales + onboarding** script: “Here’s the full list—every missing item adds days.”

## Critical path (nothing launches without these)

1. **Facebook Business Manager + Page access**  
   - Partner access to BM or admin on the **Page** that will sponsor ads.  
   - **Special Ad Category: Housing** (or applicable) — must be selected; wrong category = disapproval loops.

2. **Ad account + payment method**  
   - Either **Client’s ad account** (they pay Meta directly—cleanest for transparency) or **your BM** with their card—**spell out in SOW** who owns data.

3. **Defined territory**  
   - ZIP list or radius **from a real address** Meta can target. Vague “metro area” causes rework.

4. **Lead destination**  
   - **Starter:** Meta Lead Form may be enough day 1.  
   - **Growth+:** Landing + domain DNS + **thank-you page** + **Pixel** event = dependency chain; **domain approval** can add 48h.

5. **Primary phone + SMS path**  
   - For instant alerts; if they won’t use GHL, define **email + SMS fallback** (Zapier).

## High-impact for speed (not always “legal” blockers)

6. **3+ creative assets** — before/after, crew on roof, shingle close-up. **No assets = you can’t test hooks.**  
7. **Logo + business name** exact spelling for ads and landing.  
8. **Offer** locked: “Free roof inspection” vs “Free estimate” — changes **form + compliance** copy.  
9. **Google review count + rating** for trust strip (real numbers only).  
10. **License / disclaimer** language for **state** rules (some states require license in ad or landing).

## What slows launches most (predictable)

| Delay | Typical cause |
|-------|----------------|
| 3–7 days | Client can’t find BM admin; owner “will do it Friday” |
| 2–5 days | DNS not propagated; client used wrong host records |
| 1–2 weeks | **Ad disapprovals** — before/after claims, “insurance pays 100%,” unverifiable stats |
| Ongoing | Client **changes Page** or **disconnects** BM mid-campaign |

## What else you should build (recommended next)

| Asset | Why |
|-------|-----|
| **1 Loom library** | “How to invite us to Business Manager” (2 min)—send every client |
| **Ad policy cheat sheet** (roofing) | Approved vs risky claims; reduces disapproval cycles |
| **Disapproval response template** | Client-facing: what you need from them to appeal |
| **Lead export → Google Sheet** backup | If GHL hiccups, you still have delivery proof for **Section 6** disputes |
| **Stripe customer portal link** | Self-serve invoices = fewer “I didn’t authorize” confusions |
| **Exhibit B** in agreement | Paste **Task Sheet** so late client = documented **Go-Live** shift |

## Single metric to track internally

**Time-to-live (TTL):** payment cleared → **first ad impression**. Aim to benchmark and shrink TTL by improving **Task Sheet completion rate**.
