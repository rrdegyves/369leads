# What I need from you — website + Meta (Facebook) ads

Your **`index.html` site is already built** locally with your logo. To make it “live” and pair it with ads, here is the shortest list of **your** decisions and assets.

## A. To publish the website (choose one path)

| Path | What you need to send / do |
|------|----------------------------|
| **1. Netlify / Vercel / Cloudflare Pages (recommended)** | Create a free account; connect a **Git repo** *or* drag-drop the **`369 Leads`** folder. Custom domain optional (e.g. `369leads.ca` or your Carrd-style name). |
| **2. Your own host (cPanel, etc.)** | FTP or file upload: upload **entire** folder keeping paths (`index.html`, `styles.css`, `assets/`, `campaign/`, `deck/`, etc.). |

**From you, minimum:**

1. **Final domain name** (or use the default `.netlify.app` URL for now).  
2. **Contact links** to replace placeholders: real **email**, **Calendly** (or booking URL), optional **phone** in `index.html` and `roi-calculator.html` (`#contact` section + mailto links).  
3. **City / region copy** (optional): site is generic; for KW, we can tune the hero headline/subcopy if you want it **Kitchener–Waterloo-first** everywhere.  
4. **Logo:** site + decks + social graphics use **`assets/369-leads-logo.png`** (transparent PNG). Update that file in `assets/` when you refresh creative; optional copy at repo root as `369leads_logo.png`.

**I do *not* need:** your password for hosting—**you** connect the domain in the host dashboard after upload.

## B. Facebook Page + Business Manager (ads account)

Per your **KW Launch Guide**, you need **both**:

1. **Facebook Business Page** (public face + “Book now” / Calendly).  
2. **Meta Business Suite** at [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com): **Business portfolio**, **Ad account**, **payment method**.

**From you:**

| Item | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Business email** (not only personal) | Cleaner BM ownership long-term. |
| **Credit/debit card** for ad account | Meta bills ad spend separately from any retainer you charge clients. |
| **Legal business name** / who owns the BM | You can start as individual; update later. |
| **Page name** | e.g. `369 Leads — KW Roofing Lead Gen` or your registered name. |
| **Profile + cover images** | **Done in repo:** `assets/facebook-profile-500.jpg` (square) and `assets/facebook-cover-820x312.jpg` (upload; Meta may crop on mobile—preview in Page settings). |

**Important:** For **homeowner** roofing ads you will usually use **Special Ad Category: Housing**. Follow `operations/05-meta-special-ad-category-housing-compliance.md` when you run lead gen.
