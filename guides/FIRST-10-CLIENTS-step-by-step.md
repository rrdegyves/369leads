# Step-by-step: your first **10** roofing clients (369 Leads)

Combines your **existing outreach pack** with **2026-style** volume, multichannel discipline, and **benchmark-aware** expectations.  
**Outbound benchmarks (industry roundups, not guarantees):** cold email **reply** rates often **~1–5%** for generic sends; **tighter ICP + multichannel** can land higher; agency-style offers often **~2.5–4.5%** reply in some reports. **Plan for low conversion; win on volume + follow-up.**

---

## Phase 0 — Before day 1 of outreach (1–3 days)

1. **Minimum viable back office**  
   - Stripe (or processor) + **subscription** product for each tier  
   - Signed **MSA template** in PandaDoc / SignWell (from `legal/`)  
   - **Calendly** (15-min discovery) in email signature + DMs  

2. **Proof you can execute**  
   - `campaign/` landing **template** live somewhere (even staging)  
   - **GHL** snapshot or Carrd + Zapier path decided  
   - Read **`operations/05-meta-special-ad-category-housing-compliance.md`** once  

3. **List building**  
   - **300–500** roofers in **non-overlapping** metros (Google Maps + state license DB if available)  
   - Columns: **Company, City, Owner guess, Phone, Email, FB page URL, Google stars, # reviews, Angi? (Y/N)**  

4. **Score every row** with `outreach/08-ICP-scorecard-roofing.md` when you get signal (after first touch).

**Exit criteria:** You can send **invoice + contract** in **10 minutes** after a verbal yes.

---

## Benchmarks to internalize (contractor + your sales)

| Signal | Why it matters |
|--------|----------------|
| Roofing **CPL** public bands often **~$150–$230+** | Your **effective CPL** story vs retainer |
| **Phone / fast response** wins disproportionately | You **sell** speed-to-lead hard; **fire** clients who won’t comply |
| **Facebook/IG** often mid-pack vs referrals/SEO in channel surveys | Position Meta as **incremental pipeline**, not “replace everything” |
| **Housing Special Ad Category** | You’re **compliant** and **creative-led**—see Meta SOP |

## Phase 1 — Clients 1–3 (proof + cashflow)

**Goal:** Revenue + **case study** + **testimonial** (even if anonymized: “Regional roofer, 20+ qualified leads month 1 on Growth”).

**Pricing:** Optional **$1,500 Starter** or **~10–20% off month 1** for speed **only** if you need cash—**still** use contract + guarantee.

**Daily activity minimum (6 days/week):**

| Channel | Quota | Script source |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| FB/IG DM | 15 | `outreach/01-cold-dm-and-followups.md` |
| Cold email | 10 | `outreach/02-cold-email.md` + 4–7 follow-ups every 3–4 days |
| Cold call | 15 dials | `outreach/04-cold-call-opener.md` |
| LinkedIn | 5 connects + notes | `outreach/03-linkedin-scripts.md` |

**≈45 meaningful touches/day** + **content** (1 short post or Loom/week) pointing to **ROI calculator** + **deck**.

**Funnel math (planning only):**  
If **1%** of touches → booked call and **25%** of calls → close, then **~400 touches per close**. **10 closes ≈ 4,000 touches**—roughly **~90 days at 45/day**. Improve any stage (ICP, offer, follow-up) and touches per close drop.

**Week 1–2:** Book **10+** discovery calls (fill calendar with **volume**).  
**Calls:** `outreach/05-15-minute-sales-call-outline.md` + `06-objection-handlers.md`  
**After call:** `outreach/07-post-proposal-email.md` + correct **deck** PDF.

**By client 3:** Fill **`templates/case-study-one-pager.md`** from first win (get written approval before publishing).

---

## Phase 2 — Clients 4–7 (repeatable machine)

1. **Narrow geography** to **1–2 states** you understand (storm, licensing, creatives).  
2. **Reuse** winning DM/email lines; log **reply rate** per variant in a sheet.  
3. **Storm windows:** when hail hits, **double calls** to affected ZIPs for 48 hours.  
4. **Partners:** one **supplier rep**, one **adjuster-adjacent** intro, one **gutter/solar** shop that serves same homeowners—**1 referral lunch** per week.  
5. **Loom ads breakdown** post weekly (Meta Ad Library)—your **2026 edge** per your original playbook.

**Daily quota:** Keep **45+** touches until **calendar** is **80%** full; then maintain **25+** touches.

---

## Phase 3 — Clients 8–10 (leverage)

1. **Referral offer:** “**$500** account credit for an intro that signs **Growth+**” (adjust number).  
2. **Mini case PDF** attached to every proposal after you have **2** clients.  
3. **Raise price** on **new** territories if CPL is **under control** (optional).  
4. **Say no** to sub-**6** ICP scores—churn **kills** referrals.

---

## Tracking sheet (minimum columns)

| Date | Prospect | Channel | Touch # | Outcome (no reply / reply / call booked / proposal / won-lost) | ICP score | Notes |

**Weekly review:** Which **channel** booked the most calls? Shift **1 hour** there.

---

## What *not* to do (common failure modes)

- **Fabricate** results in DMs (chargebacks + reputation).  
- Skip **contract** for “friend” deals.  
- Ignore **Housing SAC** setup.  
- Accept **slow-call** clients without **Starter** + strict coaching.  
- Stop outreach after **client 1** (pipeline death).
